Introducing New Mexico Zia

New Mexico First Men's and Women's professional volleyball team.

New Mexico Zia

New Mexico Zia will have a men's "tier 2" team and a women's "tier 2" team. We will have a coaching staff and a practice facility. We will have at least two practices a week that works for the team. You will have complete access to a lifting gym and volleyball courts. We will run under the AAU non-profit organization for insurance and fundraising benefits it provides. The goal is to get to a tier 1 one team with enough points so everyone becomes a paid athlete. This team does not cost you anything to participate. It will just be travel if we do not fundraise enough.

About the VLA

How the VLA works is there is a 2 Tier Structure. Tier 1 is a more traditional league where teams are required to compete in Regular Season matches culminating in an end of the season Championship Tournament. Tier 2 is the stepping stone and allows teams to build their foundation for their franchise from the ground up. Unlike Tier 1, there are no competition requirements in the Tier 2. Teams are allowed the opportunity to play as much or as little as they choose. Some teams in Tier 2 are working towards Tier 1 Promotion, some teams in Tier 2 just like to play competitive volleyball and have no interest in advancing towards Tier 1. It is completely up to the organization.
